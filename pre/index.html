About Soul Vibration

Soul Vibration Clothing started with a simple idea: that getting dressed should feel good, not complicated. We're a small, independent women's wear store based on Brunswick Street, and we put the same care into our online shop as we do into our shelves.

Every piece we stock is chosen by hand. We look for pieces that wear well, last longer and feel like you — relaxed staples, soft layers and the kind of dresses you reach for again and again.
